Your Questions, Answered

Can't find what you're looking for? Reach out and our team will gladly help.

You can book online through our contact form, by email at hello@jelajahmalaysia.my, or by phone. Once we confirm availability, a 30% deposit secures your dates, with the balance due 21 days before departure. For last-minute bookings within 21 days, full payment is required at confirmation.

We accept Malaysian online banking (FPX), credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard), DuitNow QR, and bank transfer to our Maybank account. International guests can pay by card or wire transfer in MYR; your bank handles the currency conversion at checkout.

Cancellations made more than 30 days before departure receive a full refund less a small admin fee. Between 30 and 14 days, the 30% deposit is non-refundable. Within 14 days, no refund applies, though we will always try to reschedule you to another date where possible. We strongly recommend travel insurance.

Malaysia is a year-round destination, but timing depends on region. The west coast (Langkawi, Penang, KL, Melaka) is best from December to April. The east coast islands (Perhentian, Redang, Tioman) shine from March to October and close during the monsoon from November to February. Borneo is good most of the year, with drier months from March to September.

Citizens of most countries, including the UK, US, EU, Australia and ASEAN nations, enjoy visa-free entry for stays of up to 30 to 90 days. All visitors must complete the Malaysia Digital Arrival Card (MDAC) online within three days before arrival. Please always confirm current requirements with your nearest Malaysian embassy, as rules can change.
