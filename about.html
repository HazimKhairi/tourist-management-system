Since 2014

Born in Malaysia, Built for Travellers

Jelajah Malaysia began with a simple idea: that the best way to see this country is through the eyes of the people who call it home. Founded in Kuala Lumpur in 2014 by a small group of friends who grew up island-hopping in the east coast and trekking the highlands of Cameron, we set out to share the Malaysia we love, not the rushed, tick-the-box version, but the real one.

Today we are a passionate local team of planners, guides and storytellers spread across Peninsular Malaysia, Sabah and Sarawak. We speak Bahasa Melayu, English, Mandarin and Tamil, and we know which warung serves the best nasi lemak before sunrise and which jetty catches the most golden sunset. Every itinerary we build is rooted in genuine local knowledge and a deep respect for the places and communities we visit.

From a couple's honeymoon in Redang to a family expedition through the Borneo rainforest, we treat each journey as if it were our own, because to us, hospitality isn't a service. It's who we are.
